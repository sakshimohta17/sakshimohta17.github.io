Research Publication

IJASEAT · Volume 12, Issue 2 · April 2024 · Peer-Reviewed Journal

Computational Drug Repurposing to Treat Schizophrenia Using Network Medicine

Sakshi Mohta & Pravir Kumar — Molecular Neuroscience & Functional Genomics Lab, Delhi Technological University

Applied a computational network medicine pipeline to identify drug repurposing candidates for schizophrenia — one of the most treatment-resistant neurological conditions. Constructed PPI and drug-target interaction networks from 160+ SZ-associated genes, then performed Connectivity Map analysis against 9 FDA-approved antipsychotics to prioritize candidates with high drug-transcriptome similarity (tau > 90). Candidates validated against clinical trial evidence on ClinicalTrials.gov.
